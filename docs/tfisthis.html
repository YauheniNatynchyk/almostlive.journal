TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 Review

The TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 is a compelling option for Linux enthusiasts seeking a lightweight, powerful, and Linux-optimized laptop. It blends performance with portability, making it an excellent choice for developers, tech professionals, and open-source advocates.
Build and Design
The Pulse 14 Gen3 features a sleek, minimalist design with a magnesium alloy chassis, ensuring durability while keeping the weight at just 1.1 kg. The laptop sports a 14-inch QHD+ display (2880x1800) with a 16:10 aspect ratio, offering vibrant colors and sharp visuals, ideal for coding, multimedia, and general use.
Performance

Under the hood, the Pulse 14 - Gen3 is powered by AMD's Ryzen 7 7840HS processor paired with integrated Radeon 780M graphics. This setup delivers robust performance for multitasking, compiling code, and even light gaming. The device supports up to 64GB of LPDDR5X-6400 RAM, ensuring smooth operation even with demanding applications.
Storage and Battery Life
With options for PCIe 4.0 SSD storage up to 8TB, the Pulse 14 offers both speed and ample space for all your files. The 60Wh battery provides a solid balance between performance and battery life, delivering around 8-10 hours of usage depending on the workload.
Keyboard and Trackpad
The keyboard is well-designed with good key travel and a backlight, making it comfortable to type on in various lighting conditions. The trackpad is responsive and supports multi-touch gestures, although Linux users might prefer using an external mouse for more precision.
Connectivity
The laptop is well-equipped with a variety of ports, including USB-C (with Power Delivery and DisplayPort), USB-A, HDMI, and a microSD card reader. This makes it versatile for connecting to multiple peripherals and external displays.
Software and Linux Compatibility
One of the standout features of the TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 is its flawless Linux compatibility. The hardware is optimized for Linux, and it comes pre-installed with TUXEDO OS, a Linux distribution based on Ubuntu. This ensures a smooth out-of-the-box experience, though users can easily install their preferred distro without compatibility issues.
Final Thoughts
The TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 is an excellent choice for users who prioritize Linux compatibility without compromising on performance or portability. Its strong hardware, paired with a focus on Linux, makes it a top contender in the Linux laptop market.

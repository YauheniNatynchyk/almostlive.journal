TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 Review

The TUXEDO Pulse 14 - Gen3 is an excellent choice for Linux enthusiasts seeking a lightweight, powerful, and Linux-optimized laptop. It perfectly balances performance with portability, making it ideal for developers, tech professionals, and open-source advocates.

Build and Design

The Pulse 14 Gen3 features a sleek, minimalist design with a magnesium alloy chassis, ensuring durability while keeping the weight at just 1.1 kg. The 14-inch QHD+ display (2880x1800) with a 16:10 aspect ratio offers vibrant colors and sharp visuals, making it perfect for coding, multimedia, and everyday use.

Performance

Powered by AMD's Ryzen 7 7840HS processor paired with integrated Radeon 780M graphics, the Pulse 14 Gen3 delivers robust performance for multitasking, compiling code, and even light gaming. It supports up to 64GB of LPDDR5X-6400 RAM, ensuring smooth operation even with demanding applications.

Storage and Battery Life

With PCIe 4.0 SSD storage options up to 8TB, the Pulse 14 offers both speed and ample space for all your files. The 60Wh battery provides a solid balance between performance and battery life, offering around 8-10 hours of usage depending on your workload.

Keyboard and Trackpad

The keyboard is well-designed, with good key travel and a backlight, making it comfortable to type on in various lighting conditions. The trackpad is responsive and supports multi-touch gestures, though Linux users might prefer using an external mouse for added precision.

Connectivity

This laptop is well-equipped with a variety of ports, including USB-C (with Power Delivery and DisplayPort), USB-A, HDMI, and a microSD card reader. This makes it versatile for connecting to multiple peripherals and external displays.

Software and Linux Compatibility

One of the standout features of the TUXEDO Pulse 14 Gen3 is its flawless Linux compatibility. The hardware is optimized for Linux, and it comes pre-installed with TUXEDO OS, a Linux distribution based on Ubuntu. This ensures a smooth out-of-the-box experience, though users can easily install their preferred distro without any compatibility issues.

Final Thoughts

The TUXEDO Pulse 14 Gen3 is an excellent choice for users who prioritize Linux compatibility without compromising on performance or portability. Its strong hardware, paired with a focus on Linux, makes it a top contender in the Linux laptop market.
